# E-Commerce Sales Analysis - SQL Project

## 📊 Project Overview
Analyzed Q1 2024 sales data for an online software retailer to identify revenue optimization opportunities.

## 🎯 Key Findings
- **$121K total revenue** from 10 transactions
- **Software License = 53% of revenue** - clear product winner
- **Top 3 customers drive 45%** of total revenue  
- **February revenue +69%** vs January - strong growth
- **43% repeat rate** - below 60% industry benchmark

## 💡 Business Recommendations
1. **Product Strategy:** Focus marketing on Software License (53% revenue share)
2. **Customer Success:** Launch VIP program for $20K+ accounts
3. **Sales Training:** Provide support to 2 underperforming reps with zero sales
4. **Retention:** Implement customer success program to improve 43% repeat rate

## 🛠️ Technical Skills
- Complex SQL JOINs
- Window functions for ranking
- Date-based aggregations
- Customer segmentation analysis

## 📈 Business Impact
Identified $25K revenue opportunity through improved customer retention
(43% → 60% repeat rate × $121K = $20.6K additional revenue)

## 📂 Dataset
- 10 transactions, 7 unique customers, 3 products
- Time period: January - February 2024
